What is Our Money?


Our Money is an online community finance platform where those who need finance meet those who can provide it. Businesses and Not for Profits can finance projects with loans. Not-for-profits can also finance projects with donations. We want to create a community of people, businesses and not for profit organisations that fund each other's growth and development.

Who backs Our Money?


You can view our team here. Our Money is financed by a group of committed private investors. Our Money has also received financial support from Enterprise Ireland.

Why do people use the Our Money community lending platform as opposed to a bank?


Our Money allows premium borrowers to get access to credit faster than traditional banks. Borrowers also get a better interest rate as the lending community determines the interest rate appropriate to each loan application. Funders view the loans they are funding as a new asset class. Instead of having their money sitting in the bank making about 1% per annum they can fund loans in their community and get a great return. Our Money competes with the mainstream banks, building societies and credit unions to attract excellent borrowers and committed funders.
